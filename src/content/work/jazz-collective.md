# Jazz Collective

Hey! We are **Jazz Collective**.

We are a group of musicians based in Boston who love playing jazz together.  
Whether it's bebop, blues, or funk, we love to bring the energy!

Our sets move between **original music and classic standards**. No matter the set, we aim to have fun, listen to each other, and let the improvisation take things somewhere interesting.

You can find us performing around Boston, recording music, and showing up at community events.

## Community & Events

We also play for community events around Boston.

That includes **graduation ceremonies, local gatherings, and neighborhood celebrations**. Music is always better when it brings people together.

The group also helped out with a **food drive for the Allston Brighton Food Pantry**. Playing music while supporting the local community felt like the right combination.

## Workshops & Musical Growth

Members of Jazz Collective have taken part in workshops with jazz artist **Christian Scott aTunde Adjuah**.

Those sessions focused on modern improvisation, listening deeply, and exploring where jazz can go next.

## Sound

If you come to a show, you will probably hear:

- Bebop improvisation  
- Funk grooves  
- Blues inspired phrasing  
- Classic jazz standards  
- Original compositions  

All played with the laid back energy of a **Boston jazz group that enjoys making music together**.
